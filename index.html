About Us

I am a highly skilled artist, backed by industry qualifications and years of experience.

I am trained with a degree from the OZA Art School in Russia.

Luminous Nails is on the Sunshine Coast, providing all nail services including acrylic, dipping/SNS powder, hard gel/ soft gel, polygel, manicure and Shellac/gel colour.

As a managing director, I have ensured that Luminous Nails has become one of the best nail salons located in South Queensland.

There is no rushing at Luminous Nails. I take my time and get the job done properly and safely. I provide the highest quality service with the best long-lasting result.
